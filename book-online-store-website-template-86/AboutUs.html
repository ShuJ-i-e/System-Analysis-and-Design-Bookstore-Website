About Us

Power Ranger Bookstore is established in January 1984, and it has become a household name in Malaysia and has now grown to more than 50 stores nationwide. It is recognised and listed by the Malaysian Book of Records as the largest bookstore chain in Malaysia, with over 800,000 square feet of retail space.

Power Ranger Bookstore sells a wide variety of fiction, non-fiction and general interest books in English, Chinese and Malay languages, as well as school textbooks and revision books. In addition, it also offers a large selection of magazines, stationery, multi-media products, gift items and CDs.
